ANGELCOPY — QUICK GUIDE
=======================

COPY & PASTE
  1. Copy (Ctrl+C) or cut (Ctrl+X) files/folders as usual.
  2. Paste in the destination folder:
     • Ctrl+V — AngelCOPY takes over automatically, or
     • right-click → "Paste FAST (AngelCOPY)".
  Cut + paste = fast move.

DRAG & DROP
  Drag files/folders with the RIGHT mouse button onto the target:
  "Copy here FAST" / "Move here FAST" / "Mirror here FAST".
  (Left-drag stays with Windows — there is no supported hook for it.)

MIRROR
  The destination becomes an exact copy of the source: new and changed files
  are copied, anything extra at the destination is DELETED. A dialog shows
  exactly what will be copied and deleted first — Cancel is the default.

DELETE
  Selection → right-click "Delete FAST" or Shift+Del.
  Permanent, NO Recycle Bin — it always asks first.

THE PROGRESS DIALOG
  • Green stretches in the bar = skipped (already up to date at the target).
  • Speed and time remaining count only data actually copied.
  • "Keep window open when done": the dialog stays with statistics including
    the total duration. The choice is remembered globally.
  • On conflicts AngelCOPY asks: Replace / Only if newer / Skip existing.

WINDOWS 11
  The entries live under "Show more options" (Shift+F10) — unless the
  classic menu option was enabled during installation.

This guide any time: Start menu → AngelCOPY → "AngelCOPY Guide"
